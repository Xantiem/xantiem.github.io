Chilli oil

Ingredients:

  • 4-5 garlic cloves
  • 6 peppers
  • Handful chilli flakes
  • Salt
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger
  • 1-2 stalks of green onion
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce

Method:

  1. Mince all and put in heatproof bowl.
  2. Heat up oil over low-medium heat (test heat by throwing in garlic and if its ready it will immediately bubble).
  3. Pour hot oil over mixture.
