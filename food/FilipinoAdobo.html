Filipino Adobo

Ingredients:

  • 1kg Pork Belly
  • 8 minced garlic cloves
  • 0.5 cups of soy sauce
  • 3 bay leaves
  • 0.3 cups of white or cane vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon whole black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon cooking oil
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar (optional)

Method:

  1. Marinate the pork in minced garlic and soy sauce for at least 1 hour.
  2. Sear the pork til browned, add the remaining marinade, bay leaves, peppercorns and fill with water until the pork is covered. Add vinegar after a few minutes of simmering.
  3. Cover and leave on a low boil for approximately an hour.
  4. Leave open for the last 15 minutes for the sauce to reduce
  5. Serve: Serve hot with rice. :D
