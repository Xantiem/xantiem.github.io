Coconut Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 Onion
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Coconut milk

Method:

  1. Prepare rice, put 1/2 water to 1/2 coconut oil (or less, you do not want it too thick).
  2. Cut an onion into 4 and add to the rice along with sugar and salt.
  3. Prepare the rice as normal.
  4. Serve with ကြက်သားဟင်း (Kyet Thar Hin)
